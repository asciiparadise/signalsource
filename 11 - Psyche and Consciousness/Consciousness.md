# Consciousness

## Why It Belongs Here

Consciousness belongs near the centre of Signal Source because every other branch eventually passes through it.

Music, symbols, dreams, trauma, memory, occult systems, mathematics, film, landscape, religion, conspiracy, nostalgia — none of these matter without a mind experiencing them.

That sounds obvious, but it is easy to forget.

The vault is not only about objects out in the world.

It is about what those objects do inside awareness.

## What We Actually Know

Consciousness is one of the hardest problems in philosophy, psychology and neuroscience.

At the simplest level, it refers to subjective experience: the fact that there is something it feels like to be aware, to perceive, to remember, to imagine, to suffer, to dream, to listen.

There are many theories of consciousness, and the vault should not pretend to solve the problem.

## Why It Matters to Art

Art often works by changing consciousness.

A record can alter the atmosphere of a room. A film can make time feel unstable. A book can make the self feel fragmented. A painting can make history feel physically present.

That is why consciousness is not just a science topic here.

It is an aesthetic topic.

## Signal Source Reading

Consciousness is the receiver.

The world transmits through sound, image, memory, symbol and pattern. But nothing becomes meaningful until it enters awareness and begins to resonate there.

Maybe that is why the vault keeps returning to signals.

A signal is not complete when it is sent.

It becomes real when it is received.
