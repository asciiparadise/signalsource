# Psyche and Consciousness Hub

## Type
Research Hub

## Purpose

A dedicated area for the human psyche, consciousness, dreams, perception, memory, trauma, identity and altered states.

This section connects psychology, philosophy, neuroscience, mythology, art and spiritual systems.

It should not become only a clinical psychology folder. Its real purpose is to map how consciousness appears across Signal Source: in dreams, symbols, rituals, music, trauma, memory, hallucination, intuition, selfhood and the sense that reality may have hidden layers.
