# Altered States

## Why They Belong Here

Altered states belong in Signal Source because many works here are interested in what happens when ordinary consciousness loosens.

This can happen through dreams, grief, trauma, ritual, drugs, music, exhaustion, meditation, fear, religious experience, sensory overload or repetition.

The important thing is not only that perception changes.

It is that meaning changes with it.

## What We Actually Know

Altered states of consciousness are conditions where ordinary waking awareness is changed in noticeable ways.

They can include:

- dreaming
- trance
- meditation
- dissociation
- psychedelic states
- religious ecstasy
- panic
- sensory deprivation
- flow states
- sleep paralysis
- trauma responses

They can be psychologically meaningful, but they can also be confusing, frightening or destabilising.

## Signal Source Reading

Altered states matter because they make reality feel negotiable.

That can be beautiful.

It can also be dangerous.

A person may glimpse something meaningful, or they may become vulnerable to manipulation. This is why altered states connect equally to spirituality, art, cults, trauma and covert experiments.

The vault should keep that tension alive.
