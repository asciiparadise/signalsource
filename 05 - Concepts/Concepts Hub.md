# Concepts Hub

## Purpose

This section is for ideas that are not quite themes and not quite symbols.

They are interpretive tools: ways of reading works across the vault.

A theme is broad atmosphere, like [[Memory]] or [[Mortality]].

A symbol is an image or object, like [[Hexagon]], [[Tape]] or [[Forest]].

A concept is a mechanism. It explains how meaning is being produced.

## Why This Section Exists

The vault was starting to blur themes, symbols and concepts together.

That made the graph look rich, but also slightly messy.

This section should help keep the thinking cleaner.

## Signal Source Reading

The most interesting works in the vault are not only interesting because of what they are about.

They are interesting because of what they do.

They make the viewer decode.

They make the listener remember.

They make the reader doubt the archive.

They make symbols repeat until they feel like a system.

That is what concepts are for.
