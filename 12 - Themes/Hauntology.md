# Hauntology

## Type
Theme

## Core Idea

Hauntology is the feeling that the future promised by the past never arrived.

Not simple nostalgia, but the presence of abandoned futures, unrealised promises, obsolete media and cultural ghosts still active in the present.

## Why It Matters

Signal Source repeatedly returns to works that feel haunted by lost possibility: old educational films, public broadcasting, analogue technology, faded photographs, forgotten futures and music that feels like a memory from another timeline.

## Key Associations

- lost futures
- obsolete media
- public broadcasting
- analogue decay
- childhood television
- memory fragments
- cultural afterimages
