# Pastoral

Pastoral does not simply mean peaceful countryside.

In Signal Source it is more unstable than that. The pastoral is the dream of escape into nature, but also the suspicion that escape may become isolation, fantasy or denial.

This matters especially with Boards of Canada.

Their pastoral world is never purely innocent. A field can feel warm and haunted at the same time. A beautiful place out in the country can be refuge, memory, cult, fantasy, or trap.

## Softness and Unease

The pastoral often arrives as softness:

- open air
- grass
- sunlight
- roads
- hills
- childhood outdoors
- distant homes

But underneath that softness there can be control, withdrawal or loss.

That is what makes it interesting.

## Signal Source Reading

Pastoral beauty becomes powerful when it is not fully safe.

If it is only pretty, it disappears quickly. If there is something unresolved inside it, it starts to linger.
