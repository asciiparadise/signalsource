# Children

## Core Meaning

Memory, innocence, vulnerability, repetition, learning, lost futures.

## Why It Matters

Children are everywhere in the Boards of Canada world, but rarely in a simple or sentimental way.

A child’s voice can feel warm, playful and familiar. But when it is looped, degraded, counted, sampled or placed inside strange electronic music, it becomes something else. Not evil. Not even frightening in an obvious way. More like childhood seen from too far away.

That distance is where the unease enters.

## Childhood as Signal

In Signal Source, children often represent perception before explanation.

The world is not yet divided neatly into science, myth, fear, play, memory and dream. Everything is still close together. That is why childhood can feel so powerful in art. It is not only innocence. It is openness.

But openness also means vulnerability.

## Signal Source Reading

Children in these works are not only characters or voices.

They are symbols of a time before meaning becomes fixed. That is beautiful, but also dangerous, because anything can enter there: wonder, fear, indoctrination, memory, trauma, belief.
