# Static

## Core Meaning

Noise, interference, failed communication, hidden signal, atmosphere.

## Why It Matters

Static is one of those textures that can mean failure and presence at the same time.

It blocks the message, but it also tells you that something is happening. A signal is close. A machine is on. The air is not empty.

That double feeling is very important to Signal Source.

## Static as Atmosphere

In music and film, static often creates uncertainty.

Is something being transmitted?

Is something breaking down?

Is the interference hiding the message, or is the interference the message?

Boards of Canada, Twin Peaks, number stations, VHS, radio and public broadcasting all live near this question.
