# Antenna

## Core Meaning

Reception, sensitivity, hidden communication, tuning, listening.

## Why It Matters

An antenna is a strange object because it makes invisible things feel almost physical.

It stands there in the landscape or on a roof, waiting for signals the body cannot receive on its own. That alone makes it powerful as a symbol. It extends perception.

In Signal Source, antennas belong to the world of shortwave radio, number stations, public broadcasting, Cold War infrastructure, old televisions, desert towers and unknown transmissions.

## Tuning

The antenna is not only about broadcasting.

It is also about being tuned correctly.

That matters. A signal may already be there, but without the right receiver it remains hidden. This connects very naturally to the whole vault: art, symbols, memory and dreams may work the same way. Maybe they are not invented from nothing. Maybe they are received when attention is tuned in a certain way.
