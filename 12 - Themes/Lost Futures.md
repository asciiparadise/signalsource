# Lost Futures

## Type
Theme

## Core Idea

Every era imagines futures. Most never arrive.

Lost Futures explores the emotional residue left behind by abandoned technological, cultural and social dreams.

## Examples

- Atomic utopias
- Space-age optimism
- Information-age utopianism
- Educational optimism
- Ecological futures that never materialised
