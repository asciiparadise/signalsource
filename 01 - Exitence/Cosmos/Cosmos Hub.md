# Cosmos Hub

## Type
Research Hub

## Purpose

A dedicated area for astrology, astronomy, cosmology and the symbolic imagination of the universe.

This section should hold two layers at once:

1. The scientific cosmos: Big Bang, matter, stars, black holes, galaxies, dark matter, time, entropy.
2. The symbolic cosmos: astrology, star signs, mythic skies, sacred astronomy, cosmic order, fate, personality and meaning projected onto the heavens.

Signal Source is interested in both, but should keep the distinction clear.
