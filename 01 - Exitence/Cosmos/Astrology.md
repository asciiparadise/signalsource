# Astrology

## Evidence Status

**Symbolic / historical system, not scientific astronomy.**

Astrology belongs in Signal Source as a cultural, symbolic and mythic system. It should not be treated as scientific evidence about personality or fate.

That distinction matters.

The vault can take astrology seriously as language, history, symbolism and atmosphere without treating it as empirical science.

## Why It Belongs Here

Astrology belongs here because it is one of humanity's oldest attempts to connect the sky with inner life.

It turns planets, signs, houses and aspects into a symbolic map of character, fate, cycles and meaning. Whether or not one believes in astrology literally, the system itself is fascinating: a way of reading the cosmos as if it were speaking through pattern.

That makes it very close to Signal Source.

The sky becomes text.

The self becomes chart.

Time becomes symbolic weather.

## What We Actually Know

Astrology has ancient roots and appears in several historical traditions, including Babylonian, Hellenistic, Indian, Islamic and European forms.

Western astrology commonly uses:

- the twelve signs of the [[Zodiac]]
- planets and luminaries
- houses
- aspects
- birth charts
- transits

Astronomy and astrology were historically intertwined, but modern science separates them. Astronomy studies celestial objects physically. Astrology interprets celestial positions symbolically.

## Symbolic Function

Astrology is powerful because it gives people a symbolic grammar for time and personality.

It can describe moods, patterns, tensions, cycles and archetypal qualities. Even when not treated literally, it can function like myth: not proof, but pattern language.

That is why it connects to [[Carl Jung]], [[Symbolic Thinking]], [[Mythic Astronomy]] and [[Music of the Spheres]].

## Risk

Astrology becomes weaker when it is treated too simplistically.

A sun sign alone is not the full system. Newspaper horoscopes and internet memes flatten it. The deeper symbolic system is more complex, but the vault should still keep a clear boundary between symbolic usefulness and factual claim.

## Signal Source Reading

Astrology matters because it shows the human desire to find meaning in celestial order.

We look up and see movement.

Then we make pattern.

Then we ask whether the pattern also exists inside us.

Maybe that is projection.

Maybe that is poetry.

Maybe it is both.
