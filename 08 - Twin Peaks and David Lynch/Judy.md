# Judy

## Type
Entity

## Core Idea

For much of Twin Peaks, BOB appears to be the ultimate evil.

The Return reveals something larger.

Judy (Jowday) is an ancient force of extreme negativity that exists beyond ordinary Lodge entities. fileciteturn3file0

## First Appearance

The name first appears in Fire Walk With Me through Philip Jeffries.

For decades viewers assumed Judy was a person.

The Return transforms Judy into a cosmic principle.

## The Experiment

Part 8 strongly suggests that Judy manifests as the Experiment.

After the Trinity Test, this entity releases eggs into reality, including the orb containing BOB. fileciteturn3file0

## Sarah Palmer

One of the most influential interpretations of The Return is that Judy has become associated with Sarah Palmer.

This explains the overwhelming concentration of suffering around the Palmer family and the terrifying scenes involving Sarah in the revival. fileciteturn3file0

## Why Judy Matters

BOB represents appetite.

Judy represents annihilation.

BOB is a predator.

Judy is a condition.
