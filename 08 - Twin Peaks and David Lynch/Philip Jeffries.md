# Philip Jeffries

## Type
Character / Mythic Figure

## Core Idea

Philip Jeffries begins as an FBI agent and ends as something closer to a cosmic navigator.

His journey represents what happens when a human being spends too long exposed to the machinery behind reality.

## Fire Walk With Me

Jeffries appears unexpectedly at FBI headquarters after disappearing for years.

He speaks about mysterious meetings above a convenience store and mentions Judy.

Moments later he vanishes again.

## The Return

When Cooper finally finds Jeffries, he no longer exists in conventional human form.

He is associated with steam, electricity, machinery and time manipulation.

The physical apparatus appears to function as a containment device for a consciousness that has moved beyond ordinary reality.

## Symbolic Function

Jeffries is one of Twin Peaks' versions of the archetypal guide.

But unlike traditional guides, he has been damaged by the knowledge he possesses.
