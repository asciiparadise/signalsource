# David Lynch

## Type
Artist

## Core Idea

Lynch's work repeatedly explores the collision between ordinary reality and deeper hidden layers.

His films and television series are not primarily puzzles.

They are experiences designed to awaken intuition, feeling, dream logic and symbolic thinking.

## Recurring Themes

### The Uncanny

The familiar becomes strange.

### Duality

Light and darkness coexist.

Beneath every surface lies another reality.

### Dreams

Dreams are not escapes from reality.

They are another layer of reality.

### Hidden America

Lynch is one of the great mythologists of America.

Suburbs, diners, roads, forests and small towns become symbolic landscapes.

### Mystery

Mystery should be preserved rather than eliminated.

## Lynch and Signal Source

Lynch is important because he demonstrates that ambiguity can create meaning.

Many artists attempt to explain.

Lynch allows images, sounds and symbols to coexist without forcing resolution.
