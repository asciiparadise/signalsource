# MKUltra

## Evidence Status

**Documented covert programme.**

MKUltra should be treated differently from folklore-driven conspiracy material.

It was a real CIA programme involving mind-control research, drug experiments, behavioural manipulation and related projects during the Cold War. Some records were destroyed, which means the historical picture is incomplete, but the programme itself is not speculation.

## Why It Belongs Here

MKUltra belongs in Signal Source because it sits exactly at the uncomfortable intersection of psychology, state power, secrecy, trauma and the fantasy of controlling consciousness.

It is disturbing partly because it sounds like conspiracy fiction, but is rooted in documented history.

That matters.

The vault needs to keep this distinction clear. Not everything hidden is imaginary. Sometimes real institutions really did attempt grotesque things in secret.

## What We Actually Know

MKUltra was a CIA programme associated with experiments into interrogation, mind control, drugs, hypnosis, sensory manipulation and behavioural influence.

LSD and other substances were used in some experiments, often in ethically abusive conditions. The programme became publicly known through investigations in the 1970s, including the Church Committee era.

The full scope remains difficult to reconstruct because many records were destroyed.

## Related Operations and Neighbouring Cases

These are not all the same kind of thing, but they belong in the same hidden-history constellation.

- [[Project Stargate]] — government-funded remote-viewing research, more psychic-paranormal than chemical mind-control.
- [[COINTELPRO]] — FBI domestic surveillance and disruption programme; not MKUltra, but part of the same trust-breaking institutional landscape.
- [[Tuskegee Syphilis Study]] — medical betrayal by public institutions; not intelligence work, but crucial for understanding why official abuse is not only a paranoid fantasy.
- [[Operation Northwoods]] — rejected false-flag proposal; important because it shows that extreme plans could be formally drafted even when not executed.

## What Is Mythology

The mythology around MKUltra is larger than the documented facts.

It has become a container for many fears: brainwashing, assassins, cult programming, psychic warfare, pop-star control, hidden handlers, and the idea that identity can be engineered from the outside.

Some of that mythology is unsupported or exaggerated.

But the reason it has power is that the documented core is already disturbing.
