# Modern Art Canon

## Purpose

This note is now a status index, not a finished essay.

It maps the works that seem central to Signal Source and marks whether they have proper notes, thin notes, or no real note yet.

The vault should not pretend everything is equally developed.

## Status Key

- **Strong** — has a substantial Signal Source note.
- **Thin** — exists but needs factual and interpretive expansion.
- **Missing** — linked or discussed, but no proper note yet.
- **Provisional** — should be fact-checked or handled carefully.

## Signal Source Reading

The canon should not be a list of impressive works.

It should be a map of works that keep producing the same deep questions: memory, time, identity, symbols, mortality, hidden systems, spiritual longing and the feeling that reality has another layer beneath it.
