# Vault Audit - Structure and Cleanup

## Purpose

This is a pause note.

The vault has grown quickly, and that is both good and dangerous. Some notes are starting to feel alive. Others are just placeholders, link farms, or atmospheric prose without enough fact, context or structure.

The goal now should not be more expansion.

The goal should be consolidation.

---

# 1. Main Problems

## 1. Too Many Uncreated Links

Some hubs currently create large constellations of linked notes that do not yet exist. That makes the graph look richer than it actually is.

This is especially true in:

- [[Philosophy Hub]]
- [[Cosmos Hub]]
- [[Psyche and Consciousness Hub]]
- [[Hidden History and Conspiracy Hub]]
- [[Symbol Index Hub]]

These should be treated as planned areas, not finished areas.

## 2. Some Notes Are Mood Without Fact

A few notes have the right atmosphere but not enough grounding.

This is most obvious in:

- early Boards of Canada tape notes
- several symbol notes
- some new film notes
- some broad theme notes

The reflective style is good, but it needs a factual spine. Otherwise it becomes fog.

## 3. Boards of Canada Is Split Awkwardly

Boards of Canada currently lives partly in:

- `01 - Works/Music/`
- `06 - Boards of Canada/`
- `06 - Boards of Canada/Discography/`

This causes duplication and weak navigation.

The BoC section should be treated as a self-contained research domain.

## 4. Themes, Symbols and Concepts Are Blurred

Some notes currently filed as themes are really concepts.

Examples:

- [[Pattern Recognition]]
- [[Fan Archaeology]]
- [[Lost Media]]
- [[Sacred Technology]]
- [[Dream Logic]]
- [[The Past Inside the Present]]

These may deserve a separate `05 - Concepts/` section.

## 5. The Home Page Is Outdated

[[Home]] still reflects the early vault, before the major sections existed.

It should become the real navigation page.

## Two Discography Sections

The discography should be split into two main conceptual sections:

### 1. Official / Public Discography

Albums, EPs, singles, Peel Session, Trans Canada Highway, Reach for the Dead.

### 2. Archive / Mythology Discography

Early tapes, rare releases, Societas x Tape, fan-circulated material, lost-media objects.

This split fits the emotional reality of Boards of Canada better than a normal release list.

# 7. Rule Going Forward

Every substantial note should contain:

```markdown
## Why It Belongs Here

## What We Actually Know

## Signal Source Reading

## Connections
```

For symbols:

```markdown
## Core Meaning

## Where It Appears

## Signal Source Reading
```

For disputed historical material:

```markdown
## Evidence Status

Documented / disputed / folklore / artistic influence.
```

The reflective style should stay.

But it needs bones.
