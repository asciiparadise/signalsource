## Central Observation

Most works in Signal Source occupy more than one pole simultaneously.

The strongest works often begin in Memory, travel through Mystery, pass into Transformation and ultimately arrive at Entropy.

Examples:

- Music Has the Right to Children
- Geogaddi
- Inferno
- Twin Peaks: The Return
- The Rings of Saturn
- The Tree of Life
