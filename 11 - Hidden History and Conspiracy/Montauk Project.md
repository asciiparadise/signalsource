# Montauk Project

## Evidence Status

**Conspiracy folklore / disputed claims / artistic influence.**

The Montauk Project should not be treated as documented covert history in the same way as [[MKUltra]] or [[Agent Orange]].

It belongs in Signal Source because of its cultural and mythic force, not because its central claims are established fact.

## Why It Belongs Here

The Montauk Project belongs here because it is a perfect example of how Cold War sites, military secrecy, psychic research myths, time-travel fantasies and pop-cultural memory can fuse into one conspiracy mythology.

The story usually centres on alleged secret experiments at Camp Hero or Montauk Air Force Station on Long Island, involving mind control, time travel, teleportation, psychic amplification, children, monsters, and hidden technology.

That is already a complete Signal Source cluster.

But it needs caution.

## What We Actually Know

Montauk and Camp Hero were real military/radar sites with Cold War history.

That documented military presence gives the mythology a physical anchor.

The more extraordinary claims — time travel, interdimensional portals, psychic experiments, manufactured monsters — are not established historical fact.

## What Is Mythology

The mythology is the important part here.

Montauk functions as a container for fears about:

- abandoned military infrastructure
- secret experiments
- children and trauma
- mind control
- radar and invisible waves
- state power
- portals and time distortion
- technology becoming occult

It is one of those myths where the location becomes stronger than the evidence.

The place itself starts to feel like a transmitter.

## Signal Source Reading

The Montauk Project matters because it shows how a conspiracy can become a folk-horror system for the technological age.

Older folklore had haunted forests, fairy hills and cursed houses.

Modern folklore has radar stations, underground labs, decommissioned bases and government fences.

That does not make the claims true.

But it does make the mythology revealing.
