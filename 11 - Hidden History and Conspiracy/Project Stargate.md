# Project Stargate

## Evidence Status

**Documented government-funded psychic research programme / results disputed.**

Project Stargate belongs in a strange category.

The programme itself was real. The claims it investigated — remote viewing, psychic perception, military intelligence through paranormal means — are highly disputed and should not be treated as proven abilities.

That distinction is essential.

## Why It Belongs Here

Project Stargate belongs in Signal Source because it shows the Cold War mind turning even the paranormal into a possible weapons system.

That is almost the whole point.

The issue is not only whether remote viewing works. The more interesting Signal Source question is: what kind of historical atmosphere makes intelligence agencies spend serious money trying to weaponise psychic perception?

The answer is Cold War fear.

If the Soviets might be researching the mind, then the Americans must research the mind too.

Even the impossible becomes strategic.

## What We Actually Know

Project Stargate was a United States government research effort associated with remote viewing and possible military/intelligence uses of psychic phenomena.

Remote viewing generally refers to the claimed ability to perceive distant or hidden targets without normal sensory access.

The programme involved military and intelligence interest over several years, but the value and validity of the results remain disputed.

## What Is Mythology

The mythology around Stargate is enormous because it sits between real documents and extraordinary claims.

Psychics, military bases, hidden targets, Soviet submarines, psychic warfare, black budgets, secret rooms, blind coordinates.

Some of the institutional interest was real.

That does not make the paranormal claims true.

But the fact that they were investigated gives the mythology power.

## Signal Source Reading

Project Stargate matters because it turns consciousness into a surveillance device.

With [[MKUltra]], the state tries to alter the mind.

With Stargate, the state wonders whether the mind can become a sensor.

That is a perfect Signal Source inversion: the psyche as both target and instrument.
