# Hidden History and Conspiracy Hub

## Type
Research Hub

## Purpose

A dedicated area for covert military activity, intelligence agencies, conspiracy theories, psychological operations, experimental programs, state secrecy, Cold War paranoia and the blurred line between documented history and myth.

This section should keep a clear distinction between:

1. Documented covert activity
2. Plausible but disputed claims
3. Folklore, urban legend and conspiracy mythology
4. Artistic uses of paranoia and secrecy

Signal Source is interested in all four, but they should not be collapsed into one another.

## Working Principle

Conspiracy culture becomes powerful because it imitates pattern recognition.

Sometimes patterns reveal real abuse of power.

Sometimes they become fantasy systems that explain too much.

The vault should be interested in both the truth claims and the symbolic atmosphere.
