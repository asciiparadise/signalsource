# Music of the Spheres

## Why It Belongs Here

The Music of the Spheres belongs in Signal Source because it is one of the oldest versions of an idea the vault keeps circling:

maybe music, mathematics and the cosmos are connected.

Not metaphorically only. Structurally.

The idea is ancient, and parts of it are scientifically outdated. But symbolically it still has force. It gives a name to the feeling that harmony is not just something humans invent, but something we discover in the structure of things.

## What We Actually Know

The Music of the Spheres is associated with ancient Greek and later medieval thought, especially the Pythagorean tradition.

The basic idea is that celestial bodies move according to mathematical proportions, and that these proportions are analogous to musical harmony.

It was not usually imagined as literal audible music in the normal sense. It was more like a hidden cosmic harmony: number, movement and order expressed at the scale of the universe.
