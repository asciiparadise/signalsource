# Marcus Eoin Sandison

## Type
Artist / Person

## Role

Member of Boards of Canada.

## Core Idea

Marcus Eoin Sandison is one half of the central creative identity behind Boards of Canada.

In Signal Source, he belongs not only as a biographical figure, but as part of the larger artistic system around memory, mathematics, childhood, nature, analogue media and hidden structures.

## Signal Source Interpretation

The individual biography is less important than the shared artistic method: constructing music that feels as though it has been recovered from memory, broadcast through damaged media, and encoded with patterns that may or may not be fully decipherable.
