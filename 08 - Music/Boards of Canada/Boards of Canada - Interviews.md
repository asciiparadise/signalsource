# Boards of Canada - Interviews

## Purpose

Index and extract recurring ideas from interviews rather than collecting quotations alone.

## Recurring Themes

### Memory

Childhood memory, educational films, nostalgia, imperfect recollection and emotional memory recur throughout many interviews.

### Nature

Nature is often described as a primary influence, not as scenery but as a system of patterns and relationships.

### Mathematics

References to structure, pattern, geometry, hidden relationships and systems thinking appear repeatedly.

### Ambiguity

The group often avoids definitive explanations and prefers open interpretation.

### Technology and Humanity

A recurring tension between analogue technology, human emotion and modern digital culture.

### Symbolism

Symbols are often treated as suggestive rather than fixed in meaning.

## Research Tasks

- Extract major themes from every interview.
- Build a timeline of changing ideas.
- Track recurring concepts.
- Connect interviews to albums, symbols and themes.
