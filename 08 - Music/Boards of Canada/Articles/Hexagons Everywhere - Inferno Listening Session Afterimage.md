# Hexagons Everywhere - Inferno Listening Session Afterimage

## Type
Article / Reflection Note

## Source

User-provided text about hexagons appearing everywhere after a Boards of Canada *Inferno* listening session.

## Core Idea

After attending a listening session for *Inferno*, the writer describes suddenly noticing hexagons everywhere: clothing patterns, cinema carpet, a film image, Borges, a tech logo, a wasp nest, a car speaker grille.

The point is not necessarily that the world is secretly organised around Boards of Canada. It is that Boards of Canada primes perception. Their symbols become active filters. After exposure to their world, ordinary reality begins to look coded.

## Hexagon as Perceptual Trigger

The hexagon functions here less as a static symbol and more as an afterimage.

Once the listening session has placed the shape at the centre of attention, it becomes difficult not to see it everywhere. This resembles pattern recognition, synchronicity, paranoia, fandom, occult attention and the pleasures of interpretive overreach.

## Boards of Canada and the Hexagon

The text reinforces the privileged place of the hexagon in Boards of Canada lore:

- "Turquoise Hexagon Sun"
- Hexagon Sun as studio / collective name
- hexagonal imagery around *Geogaddi*
- the rotating flaming hexagon at the *Inferno* listening session
- venue architecture and carpet patterns

## Key Quote / Idea

The writer recalls Marcus Eoin explaining the hexagon theme as connected to seeing reality clearly: the raw mathematics and patterns beneath everything.

This is one of the most important formulations for Signal Source. The hexagon is not only a shape. It represents the idea that art, music, drugs, mathematics or altered attention can pull back the curtain and reveal the hidden operating system beneath reality.
