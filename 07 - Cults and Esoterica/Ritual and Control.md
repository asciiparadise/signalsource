# Ritual and Control

## Type
Theme

## Core Idea

Ritual can create meaning, belonging and transformation.

It can also become a mechanism of authority, hierarchy and control.

## Why It Matters

Many movements, institutions and artistic works use ritual structures whether they are religious, political or secular.
