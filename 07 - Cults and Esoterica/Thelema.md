# Thelema

## Type
Esoteric System

## Core Idea

A modern esoteric philosophy associated with Aleister Crowley.

Thelema is significant because it helped shape twentieth-century occult culture, ritual symbolism, magical thinking and modern interpretations of personal spiritual authority.

## Themes

- Will
- Ritual
- Self-transformation
- Esotericism
- Symbolism

## Cultural Influence

Thelema has influenced artists, musicians, occult movements, alternative spiritual communities and popular culture.
