# Spiral

## Core Meaning

Growth, recurrence, emergence, evolution, return.

## Why It Matters

The spiral is one of the most important natural forms.

It appears in galaxies, shells, storms, plants and mathematical growth patterns.

## Signal Source

The spiral represents movement through time while still returning to familiar territory.
