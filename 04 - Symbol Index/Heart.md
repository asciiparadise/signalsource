# Heart

## Core Meaning

Life, mortality, pulse, body, love, fear, signal.

## Why It Matters

The heart is one of the oldest symbols, but in Signal Source it should not be treated only as romance or emotion.

It is more physical than that.

A heartbeat is proof that the body is still here. It is rhythm before music, signal before language. That makes it especially powerful in works that deal with memory, death and the body slowly failing.

## Heartbeat as Signal

In *Inferno*, the heartbeat thread matters because it pulls all the religious, occult and apocalyptic imagery back into the human body.

The album may speak through fire, prophecy, cults and cosmic symbolism, but the heartbeat reminds us that underneath all of that is something fragile and biological.

The heart matters because it refuses abstraction.

No matter how cosmic or symbolic a work becomes, the heartbeat brings it back to the body. To life continuing. To life stopping.
