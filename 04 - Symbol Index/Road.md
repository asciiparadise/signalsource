# Road

## Core Meaning

Movement, distance, escape, return, memory in motion.

## Why It Matters

The road is one of the most important modern symbols.

It promises freedom, but it also creates repetition. You move forward, but the mind keeps returning to the same images. Childhood car journeys, radio signals, fields through glass, late light, unknown towns.

In Signal Source, the road is rarely just travel.

It is memory stretched across geography.

## Signal Source Reading

A road can feel like escape and haunting at the same time.

That is probably why it works so well with Boards of Canada. Their roads do not lead cleanly away from the past. They carry the past with them.
