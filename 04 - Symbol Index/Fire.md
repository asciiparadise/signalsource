# Fire

## Core Meaning

Transformation, destruction, spirit, signal, ritual, danger.

## Why It Matters

Fire is one of those symbols that never stays simple.

It can warm, purify, destroy, reveal, consume, transmit and transform. In religious and occult systems it often becomes divine presence or ritual force. In Twin Peaks it becomes almost metaphysical. In *Inferno* it becomes tied to mortality, faith and the body.

Fire is never only fire.
