# Electricity

## Core Meaning

Transmission, spirit, technology, possession, life-force, modern magic.

## Why It Matters

Electricity is one of the symbols that connects Twin Peaks and Boards of Canada most strongly.

In ordinary life, electricity is infrastructure. It powers rooms, screens, radios, machines. But in art it often becomes something stranger: invisible energy moving through the world, connecting places that should remain separate.

In *Twin Peaks*, electricity is almost spiritual. It is how entities move. It is how voices travel. It is how reality flickers.

## Technology as Spirit

There is something very modern about electricity as a mystical force.

Older myths had wind, fire, water, breath, divine light. Modern myth has wires, static, lamps, microphones, screens and sockets.

That is probably why electricity fits Signal Source so well. It is where technology stops feeling practical and starts feeling metaphysical.
