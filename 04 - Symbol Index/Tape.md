# Tape

## Core Meaning

Memory, degradation, recording, time, warmth, loss.

## Why It Matters

Tape is probably one of the most important materials in Signal Source.

It does not just record sound. It records time passing through sound. The hiss, wobble, dropout and softness are not neutral flaws. They make the past feel physical.

This is why tape is so central to Boards of Canada and William Basinski. A clean recording can feel like information. Tape feels like memory.

## Degradation as Emotion

There is something about tape damage that seems to humanise sound.

Maybe that is irrational, but it feels true. Digital failure often feels broken. Tape failure often feels aged. It bends, fades and smears instead of simply disappearing.

That softness makes loss easier to hear.
