# Philosophy Hub

## Type
Research Hub

## Purpose

A map of philosophical ideas relevant to Signal Source: ancient metaphysics, modern consciousness, time, memory, mathematics, reality, myth, language, technology, aesthetics and the question of whether the world has hidden structure.

This section should not become a generic history of philosophy.

It should focus on thinkers and ideas that connect to the vault's core concerns:

- memory
- time
- hidden systems
- sacred geometry
- music and mathematics
- consciousness
- dreams
- myth
- apocalypse
- aesthetics
- simulation
- reality as structure
