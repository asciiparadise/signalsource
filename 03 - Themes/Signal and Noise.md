# Signal and Noise

## Type
Theme

## Core Idea

The relationship between meaning and interference.

Signals carry information. Noise obscures it. Yet many artistic works derive their power from existing somewhere between the two.

## Why It Matters

A recurring theme throughout Boards of Canada, analogue media, number stations, VHS degradation and forgotten technologies.
