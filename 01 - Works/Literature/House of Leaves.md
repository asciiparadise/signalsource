# House of Leaves

## Type
Work

## Medium
Novel

## Author
Mark Z. Danielewski

## Core Idea

A labyrinth disguised as a novel.

The house becomes larger on the inside than the outside. The book itself becomes a maze. Form and content collapse into one another.
