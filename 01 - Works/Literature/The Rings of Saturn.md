# The Rings of Saturn

## Type
Work

## Medium
Literature

## Author
W. G. Sebald

## Core Idea

A wandering journey through landscapes, history, memory, ruins and melancholy.

The book moves associatively rather than narratively, allowing one subject to drift into another until an entire worldview emerges.

## Why It Matters

This may be the closest literary equivalent to the emotional territory occupied by Boards of Canada.
