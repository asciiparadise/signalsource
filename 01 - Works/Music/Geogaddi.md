# Geogaddi

## Type
Work

## Medium
Music Album

## Artist
Boards of Canada

## Core Idea

The arcadian childhood atmosphere of earlier Boards of Canada is inverted and shadowed. Childhood remains present, but now sits beside cults, apocalypse, numerology, occult symbolism, ecological anxiety and hidden systems.

## Why It Matters

Geogaddi is one of the central texts of Signal Source because it demonstrates how innocence and dread can coexist.
