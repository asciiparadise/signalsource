# The Disintegration Loops

## Type
Work

## Medium
Music

## Artist
William Basinski

## Core Idea

Magnetic tape loops physically decay while playing. The recording process documents their destruction.

The work transforms a technical failure into a meditation on memory, mortality and impermanence.

## Why It Matters

One of the defining artistic statements on time and loss.
