# The Tree of Life

## Type
Work

## Medium
Film

## Creator
Terrence Malick

## Core Idea

A film that attempts to hold childhood, grief, family, mortality, nature and the universe inside the same frame.

## Why It Matters

One of the few modern works with ambitions comparable to Twin Peaks: The Return. It treats personal memory and cosmic existence as parts of the same story.
