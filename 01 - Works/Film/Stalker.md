# Stalker

## Type
Work

## Medium
Film

## Creator
Andrei Tarkovsky

## Core Idea

A journey into a forbidden Zone gradually becomes a journey into consciousness, belief, desire and uncertainty.

The Zone is one of the most important locations in modern art. It functions simultaneously as landscape, spiritual territory, psychological projection and mythic space.

## Why It Matters

Many modern works concerned with mystery, liminality and spiritual ambiguity descend from Stalker either directly or indirectly.
