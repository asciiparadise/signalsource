# Anselm Kiefer

## Type
Artist

## Medium
Painting / Sculpture / Installation

## Core Idea

Kiefer's work is concerned with history, mythology, memory, religion, catastrophe, ruins and cultural inheritance.

His paintings often feel archaeological, as if excavated rather than painted.

## Why It Matters

Few visual artists inhabit the same territory of memory, symbolism and historical weight as Boards of Canada.
