# Big Active

## Type
Studio

## Medium
Creative Direction / Graphic Design / Visual Identity

## Core Idea

Big Active is one of the most influential contemporary design studios working in music and culture.

Their work demonstrates how a strong visual system can create mythology around artists, records and ideas.

## Why It Matters

Signal Source is interested not only in artworks themselves, but in the systems that surround them.

Big Active excels at creating:

- visual worlds
- recurring symbols
- identity systems
- conceptual coherence
- long-form visual narratives

## Signal Source Interpretation

The studio's importance lies in its understanding that identity is built through repetition.

A symbol repeated often enough becomes mythology.

A colour becomes a world.

A visual rule becomes a belief system.
