# Signal Source

Signal Source is an Obsidian vault for the things that keep returning.

Music, film, television, literature, painting, design, symbols, numbers, cults, old broadcasts, lost futures, strange places, and the feeling that certain works are connected even when they do not obviously belong together.

I do not want this to become a generic archive. That would be too easy, and probably quite dead. The point is not just to collect facts about things I like. The point is to understand why some works stay with me, why certain symbols keep appearing, and why memory, sound, mathematics, mythology and hidden systems seem to circle around each other.

A lot of this vault will probably be provisional. Maybe some connections are real. Maybe some are just pattern recognition doing what it does. But that is also part of the project.
