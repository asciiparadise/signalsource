## Purpose

A dedicated area for cults, occult systems, esoteric movements and religious fringe groups as they relate to Signal Source.

This section is not about sensationalism or belief. It is about cultural atmosphere, symbolism, charismatic authority, apocalypse, ritual structure, hidden systems and the way these ideas surface in music, film, literature and visual language.

## Working Frame

Cults and esoteric systems often appear in Signal Source not as literal belief systems, but as symbolic machines.

They provide forms, language and imagery for:

- transformation
- submission
- secrecy
- revelation
- apocalypse
- hierarchy
- initiation
- forbidden knowledge
- mythic self-narration

## Important Distinction

This section should keep historical study, artistic interpretation and ethical clarity separate.

A movement can be aesthetically or symbolically significant while still being destructive, manipulative or abusive in real life.
