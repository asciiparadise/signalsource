# Cults

## Type
Theme

## Core Idea

Cults are not defined solely by unusual beliefs.

For Signal Source, the most important questions involve structure:

- authority
- belonging
- initiation
- secrecy
- revelation
- transformation
- apocalypse

## Why It Matters

Many works in the vault use cult imagery symbolically rather than literally.

The imagery becomes a language for discussing identity, belief, community, fear and meaning.
