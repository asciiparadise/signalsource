# Aleister Crowley

## Type
Person

## Core Idea

Occultist, writer, mountaineer, provocateur and founder of Thelema.

Crowley is one of the most influential figures in modern esotericism. His importance to Signal Source lies less in belief and more in influence: symbolism, ritual, self-mythology, hidden knowledge, modern occult aesthetics and the relationship between spiritual systems and artistic culture.

## Themes

- Ritual
- Thelema
- Self-transformation
- Symbolism
- Myth-making

## Cultural Influence

Crowley's influence appears across music, counterculture, psychedelic culture, occult revival movements and modern conspiracy narratives.
