## Core Idea

Occult means hidden.

In this vault the occult is approached primarily as a cultural, symbolic, historical and artistic phenomenon rather than a system of belief.

## Why It Matters

Occult systems often function as symbolic frameworks for understanding reality, transformation, consciousness and hidden order.
