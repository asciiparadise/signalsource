## Purpose

A dedicated area for David Lynch, Twin Peaks and the wider mythic system surrounding his work.

This section is not only about plot, lore or solved mysteries. It is about atmosphere, spiritual geography, American mythology, dream logic, evil, electricity, time, memory, trauma, thresholds and the way Lynch turns ordinary places into charged symbolic spaces.

## Working Frame

Twin Peaks works because it refuses to remain one thing.

It is:

- soap opera
- murder mystery
- cosmic horror
- small-town Americana
- spiritual drama
- dream transmission
- trauma narrative
- mythic landscape

The myth grows from the tension between the ordinary and the cosmic.
