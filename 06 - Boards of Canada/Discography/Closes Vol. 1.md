# Closes Vol. 1

## Artist

[[Boards of Canada Hub|Boards of Canada]]

## Release Type

Early tape / rare release

## Why It Matters

*Closes Vol. 1* belongs to the hidden beginning of Boards of Canada.

Like the other early tapes, it has that strange status where the title may be more present in the mythology than the music itself. It exists as an index entry, a rumour, a signal from before the project became publicly legible.

That might sound like a weakness, but for Boards of Canada it becomes part of the atmosphere.

## The Private Archive

There is something compelling about the idea of a private archive before the official world begins.

A place where sounds, images, jokes, samples, melodies and obsessions are still unarranged. Before they become albums. Before they become themes. Before they become mythology.

*Closes Vol. 1* sits in that space.
