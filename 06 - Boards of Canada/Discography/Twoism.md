## Core Idea

*Twoism* is the threshold between the hidden early-tape mythology and the publicly visible Boards of Canada catalogue.

It matters because it already contains much of the later emotional grammar: woozy melody, analogue warmth, memory haze, childlike unease, pastoral melancholy and a sense of private ritual.

## Why It Matters

*Twoism* shows Boards of Canada beginning to crystallise as an aesthetic world rather than only a musical project.

The sound is still partly raw and private, but the emotional architecture is already clear.
