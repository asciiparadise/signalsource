# Old Tunes Vol. 2

## Artist

[[Boards of Canada Hub|Boards of Canada]]

## Release Type

Early tape / rare release

## Why It Matters

*Old Tunes Vol. 2* belongs with *A Few Old Tunes*, but it has its own kind of ghostliness.

These early collections feel like looking through a drawer of unsorted photographs. Some are clear, some are damaged, some seem insignificant until you realise they carry the emotional DNA of the later work.

That is why they matter.

They are not just extras.

They are the background radiation of Boards of Canada.

## Fragmentary Memory

The power of these tapes comes from their looseness.

There are pieces that feel like sketches, jokes, studies, accidents, private experiments. But because we hear them after the later albums, they take on a strange retrospective glow.

We start hearing origins everywhere.

Maybe that is dangerous, but it is also part of the pleasure.

## Signal Source Reading

This is Boards of Canada before the symbols become stable.

The atmosphere is already there, but still scattered. That makes it valuable in a different way from the albums. It feels like catching the myth before it knows it is a myth.
