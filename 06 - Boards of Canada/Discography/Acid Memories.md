# Acid Memories

## Artist

[[Boards of Canada Hub|Boards of Canada]]

## Release Type

Early tape / rare release

## Why It Matters

*Acid Memories* feels important almost because most of us cannot really hold it properly.

It sits at the edge of the Boards of Canada archive, more like a rumour with a title than a normal release. That gives it a strange power. Maybe the music itself would feel smaller if it were fully available, fully documented, fully placed in order. But as it stands, it belongs to the half-lit part of the mythology.

The name itself is perfect: acid, memory, distortion, chemistry, recollection. It already sounds like a Signal Source object.

## The Myth of the Missing Beginning

Every mythology needs an origin that is partly hidden.

For Boards of Canada, the earliest tapes perform that function. They suggest that before the public records, before the artwork, before the interviews and fan analysis, there was already a private world forming in the background.

*Acid Memories* is part of that imagined beginning.
