# Boards of Canada Mythology

## Core Idea

Boards of Canada is unusual because the mythology surrounding the work has become almost as important as the music itself.

The mythology emerges from the interaction between:

- the music
- recurring symbols
- scarce interviews
- hidden references
- fan interpretation
- archival recovery
- deliberate ambiguity

## Major Mythic Components

### Hexagon Sun

The central symbolic node.

A studio name, collective identity and recurring symbol linking mathematics, nature and hidden structure.

### Educational Media

Public broadcasting, school films, educational graphics and childhood television function almost like sacred texts within the Boards of Canada universe.

### Lost Tapes

The early tape releases occupy a semi-legendary position.

Many listeners encounter them as degraded copies, fragments or recovered signals.

### Hidden Systems

Repeated references to:

- numbers
- patterns
- broadcasts
- coded messages
- geometry
- coincidence

encourage interpretive participation.

### Fan Archaeology

Boards of Canada culture includes a tradition of investigation, recovery, cataloguing and interpretation.

The mythology grows through collective attention.
